I'm a PhD candidate in Computer Science at UC San Diego, advised by Prof. Julian McAuley.

I like researching and developing machine learning applications to enhance user experience. This includes recommender systems, information retrieval, prediction models, and many other applications within the broader scope of data mining and natural language processing.

Since the introduction of LLMs, my research has centered on exploring their capabilities and integrating them into innovative applications, such as conversational recommendation and natural language generation.
